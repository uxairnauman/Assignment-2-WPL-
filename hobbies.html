How we unplug

Creative breaks keep our code sharp. Here is how each teammate recharges outside the classroom.

Weekly priorities

  1. Early morning trail runs to reset focus.
  2. Two-hour studio block for sketching UI ideas.
  3. Weekend co-op gaming session with friends.
  4. Reading club dedicated to design leadership.
  5. Volunteering at the campus makerspace.

Spontaneous joys

  • Urban sketching and watercolor experiments.
  • Brewing third-wave coffee recipes.
  • Speed-solving mechanical puzzles.
  • Exploring farmers markets for fresh ingredients.
  • Hosting low-fi music listening parties.
